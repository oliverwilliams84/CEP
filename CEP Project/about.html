What We Do

Families in Care provides an independent advocacy and counselling for parents and other family members involved with child protection services across Tyneside. We accept referrals from concerned professionals as well as service users themselves. Please contact us for more details...

Why We Do It

The simple answer is - because people tell us that they need our service...

And because we are passionate about it.

Our History

Families in Care started as a self help group providing support for isolated and vulnerable birth parents. It then, in 1992, developed into a a registered charity, which over 20 years has been providing dedicated advocacy, support and counselling service for birth families involved with child protection services.

Now our agency is higly valued by parents and professionals working in the child protection field across Tyneside.

Research confirms that FIC addresses a significant gap in provision, ‘being the only organisation in Newcastle that offers specialist advocacy for parents with learning disabilities whose children are involved in Child Protection Proceedings…

(Parents with learning disabilities in Newcastle upon Tyne,’ Barefoot Research and Evaluation 2012).
