Independent Advocacy

Families in Care provides an independent advocacy service to birth families involved with child protection services. This includes one-to-one appointments, support over the phone, assisstance during professional meetings and in court.

Counselling

The child protection process and care proceedings can be very stressful for all family members. Families in Care provides a confidential counselling service to assist during and after this difficult time.

Peer Support Groups

Peer support groups offer an excellent opportunity to meet other parents who went through child protection and / or care proceedings. It is free, safe and confidential space for people to share experiences and get advice.
