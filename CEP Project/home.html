What we do

Families in Care provides an independent advocacy and counselling for parents and other family members involved with child protection services across Tyneside. We accept referrals from concerned professionals as well as service users themselves. Please contact us for more details.

Why we do it

The simple answer is - because people tell us that they need our service.

And because we are passionate about it.

Where we work

Families in Care is based in Newcastle and provides direct service on outreach basis in Tyneside area. Please contact us for more information.

Our team

Families in Care employs dedicated qualified social workers and a counsellor. We are grateful for receiving help from our passionate volunteers who dedicate their skills and time to enhance our work in counselling, advocacy and therapeutic activities fields.

Independent Advocacy

Families in Care provides an independent advocacy service to birth families involved with child protection services. This includes, for example, one-to-one appointments, support over the phone, assisstance during professional meetings and in court.

Counselling

The child protection process and care proceedings can be very stressful, even traumatic, for all family members. Families in Care provides a confidential counselling service to assist during and after this difficult time.
