What we will do with your money?

  • We will provide vital support to birth parents when they most need it. It will involve independent advocacy, support and professional counselling.
  • Birth parents will be able to continue to participate in therapeutic group work and mindfulness.
  • Parents in need of advice and information will be able to continue to access Families in Care sessions.
  • We will help birth parents to develop skills and to gain knowledge necessary to share their experiences.
  • There is so much more that we can do - but we will not be able to make it happen without your help.
  • Contact us and we will be more than happy to tell you more about our work.
