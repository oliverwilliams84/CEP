Peer Support Groups


Peer Support Sessions

Parents involved with children social services can access our peer support sessions, where they can meet other parents in similar situation. The groups offer safe space where people can come together for activities, a cup of tea, to share their experiences and swap advices.

There are currently two support groups running:

  • Crafts and Chatter for women and men - once a week
  • Women only support group - once a month

There are no special requirements to access our group, however you must be able to adhere to our ground rules - such as confidentiality, boundaries and showing respect to each other.

These are not drop-in groups - please contact us for more information.
